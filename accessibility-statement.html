Accessibility statement

I am committed to making this website accessible and usable for as many people as possible.

This website aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA and has been designed to work with assistive technologies and to be navigable using a keyboard.

Some content, such as images of user flows or design artefacts, may not be fully accessible to all users. Where this is the case, there are alternative descriptions or summaries.

If you have difficulty using this website or need information in a different format, please email me at ashley@ashleywheat.design.
