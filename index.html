What I do

I help organisations understand their users. I design usable, accessible services and help teams make design decisions based on research.

Interaction design

I design intuitive, accessible services based on user needs. This includes wireframes and working, coded prototypes that can be tested with users. I am an expert in the GOV.UK and NHS Design Systems.

User research

I help teams understand their users and their needs using interviews, usability testing, surveys, card sorting, tree testing and analytics. I turn findings into actionable insights and recommendations.

UX consultancy

I work with teams to identify usability problems, improve journeys, and shape the UX direction of a service. I carry out reviews and assessments, and support teams as they plan and deliver meaningful changes.

Service design

I look at how users move through a service across every touchpoint. I map journeys, identify pain points, and support teams in designing services that meet user needs and work end-to-end.
